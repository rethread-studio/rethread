Megatouch

by Håkan Lidbo & Per-Olov Jernberg

Megatouch is a 6×1 meter touch surface where visitors can discover hundreds of sounds, rhythms and animations, all perfectly synchronized. It is a collaborative musical instrument and visualizer that requires no prior knowledge. The size and complexity opens for music exploration rather than music mastery. It is also a reflection on the fact that music is invisible. But with Megatouch, each sound and melody has its own abstract visual expression. Megatouch is created with Pure Data with 3 synchronised computers, 3 85" screens, 6 loudspeakers or 12 headphones.

Megatouch will be exhibited between 04/10 and 12/10.

about the artists

Håkan Lidbo is an artist, musician and innovator and Per-Olov Jernberg is a polyglot coder and creative technologist. Driven by curiosity of how technology and code can be used if thinking different, they explore new forms of interaction, design new (interactive) installations and new ideas.
