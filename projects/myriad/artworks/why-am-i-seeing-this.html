Why Am I Seeing This?

by Ivana Tkalčić

Why Am I Seeing This? is a video installation, using four synchronized videos powered by Raspberry Pi, explores the influence of Information, Communication, and Technologies (ICT) on human perception and reality. It examines how digital landscapes, algorithms, and personalized news feeds create "parallel information universes," isolating users within ideological bubbles. Collaborating with scientists from the European Commission, the project questions ICT's role in shaping perception, challenging traditional distinctions between object and subject. It critiques the effects of online interaction, data ownership, and the evolution of human vision in a hyperconnected era, questioning free choice and control over personal data.

about the artist

Ivana Tkalčić, born in 1987, is a multimedia artist and researcher. She earned degrees in economics from the Faculty of Economics and Business and in fine arts from the Academy of Fine Arts Zagreb, University of Zagreb as well as graduated from WHW Akademija. Tkalčić has exhibited solo and in group events across Europe and globally. She has received numerous nominations and awards including the Radoslav Putar Award, HPB Grand Prix, and RCAA. Her art residencies span countries like Italy, Belgium, Norway, and the U.S., with notable ones including 2023 Art Omi in NY and 2018 V2_ Lab in Rotterdam.
