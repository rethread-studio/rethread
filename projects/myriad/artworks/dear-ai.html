Dear Ai

by Fred Wordie

Dear Ai is an exhibition piece and part of an ongoing project that investigates the topic of technology's mediation of human relationships. Building on the project's first output, a piece of design fiction (dearai.xyz), this installation displays a collection of letters penned by a robot in human-like handwriting, each once both deeply personal and also completely devoid of any humanness. A robot at the center of the display writes new letters in real-time, challenging viewers to consider the trade-offs between efficiency and visceral human connections. Is "I miss you" scrawled on the back of a postcard worth 5000 words of AI love?

Dear Ai will be exhibited between 4/10 and 12/10.

about the artist

Fred Wordie is an artist, critical designer, and creative technologist who uses design fiction, speculative design, and critical design to critique emerging technology's impact on society. His current research focuses on AI as a form of Techno-Socialism and its effects on personal and societal levels. He examines how Generative AI reinforces power structures and dehumanizes through technomorphism.
