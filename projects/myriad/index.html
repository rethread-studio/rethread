myriad

communities and digital art(ists)

myriad explores the themes of origin, contribution, inspiration and accreditation in human expression and creation, through the example of digital artworks and the software used to create them.

The project consists of two main components.

an exhibition: a symposium:
- A not so distant past, Steve Ashby - 4 panel, to be announced soon
- Apocryph, Nicolas Boillot
- Dear Ai, Fred Wordie
- Glommen, Jonas Johansson
- Infinite Sand Sorter, Agoston Nagy
- loam, re|thread
- Megatouch, Håkan Lidbo & Per-Olov Jernberg
- Pain Creatures, nar.interactive
- RELAXRELAXRELAX, Yuvia Maini
- Why Am I Seeing This?, Ivana Tkalčić

Opening October 4th 2024 at the KTH Reactor Hall, in Stockholm, Sweden.
