myriad

a re|thread project

myriad explores the themes of origin, contribution, inspiration and accreditation in human expression and creation, through the example of digital artworks and the software used to create them.
The first iteration of the project consisted of an exhibition, open from 05/10/24 to 12/10/24, at the R1 Reactor Hall, in Stockholm, Sweden. See the documentation here.

photo: @glidephotos - Martynas Justinevicius

10 artworks were exhibited:
- loam, by re|thread, displays the credits of the other 9 pieces:
- A not so distant past, by Steve Ashby
- Apocryph, by Nicolas Boillot
- Dear Ai, by Fred Wordie
- Glommen, by Jonas Johansson
- Infinite Sand Sorter, by Agoston Nagy
- Megatouch, by Håkan Lidbo & Per-Olov Jernberg
- Pain Creature, by nar.interactive
- RELAXRELAXRELAX, by Yuvia Maini
- Why Am I Seeing This?, by Ivana Tkalčić

Parallel to the exhibition, a symposium, core and chorus, was held on the 06/10/24.
