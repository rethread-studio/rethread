The world-wide web (www) turns 30!

Pellow is an interactive installation that reveals what happens under the hood of a browser.

Browse the web at your convenience. You can search on Google, go to your favorite web sites or click the logos below. Do as you would do with your personal device.

Installationen har fått sitt namn efter Nicola Pellow. Hon är en internetpionjär som 1991 programmerade den andra webbläsaren som någonsin skapats: Line Mode Browser. Mer om Pellow
This installation is named after Nicola Pellow. She is an Internet pionner who programmed the second web browser ever created, in 1991: the Line Mode Browser. More about Pellow
